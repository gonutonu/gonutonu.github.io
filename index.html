Hamare Bare Mein (About Us)

PM SHRI +2 High School Pratappur Lakhisarai zila ke Halsi block ka ek prachin aur garvshali shikshan sansthan hai. Iski sthapna 1978 mein hui thi. Aaj ye school PM SHRI scheme ke tehat ek 'Exemplar School' ke roop mein vikasit ho raha hai.

Humara staff bacchon ke sarvangeen vikas ke liye pratibadh hai, jisme digital learning aur modern pedagogy ka mukhya yogdaan hai.
